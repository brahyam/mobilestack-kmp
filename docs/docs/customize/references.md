# References

Replace MobileStack references with your product name.

## Links

In the `ProfileComponent.kt` file, replace all the links and emails with your own.

```kotlin
        osCapabilityProvider.openUrl("mailto:YOUR_EMAIL")
        osCapabilityProvider.openUrl("YOUR_WEBSITE")
```

## Copy

Search for references to `MobileStack` in the `shared/commonMain/kotlin/.../ui/view` folder and replace them with your product name.
